Proxy configuration

Choose how Bavarium connects for browsing: local Ultraviolet or Scramjet.

Scramjet is faster but may not work all the time. Ultraviolet is slower but usually works on most sites. Tab names are not available while the proxy is enabled.

Epoxy is recommended and faster than Libcurl. Libcurl is for maximum compatibility if sites you use are breaking or are strict.

WSS servers provide a persistent, secure connection to the websites you connect through.

It is recommended you choose a port above 10000 to avoid conflicts with other commonly used ports. Your choice of port does not affect how proxies perform.

Bavarium will use the system network settings instead of your selected proxy. It is not recommended you disable this.

History/Privacy

Control whether visits are remembered, manage site permissions (location, notifications, camera, and more), and review saved history.

Choose what sites may access (location, notifications, camera, clipboard, and similar). “Ask every time” prompts when a site requests access. Rules apply to normal browsing tabs, not Incognito. When the proxy is on, Bavarium maps permissions to the real site (e.g. https://store.steampowered.com), not localhost. You can add a site manually with its normal URL if it has not appeared yet.

Removes saved browsing history, the download history list, site permission rules, bookmarks, and all site data for this profile (cookies, cache, local storage, service workers, and similar). Your settings on this page are kept. Signed-in sessions on sites will be signed out.
